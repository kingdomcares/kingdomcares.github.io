Kingdom Cares International

...is non-profit organization with work focused both in Ghana, Africa and Des Moines, Iowa. In Ghana our outreach improves the lives of many in the village of Asikuma, where the majority of our Ghana outreach is done. In Iowa, our outreach is through our basketball programming. Both of these outreach methods provide platforms for being the hands and feet of Christ by serving others and sharing the gospel.
